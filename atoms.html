Atoms

Atoms are the smallest unit of matter that have properties of an individual element. Every atom has a nucleus, containing protons and neutrons, and a electrons. Protons have what is refered to as a positive charge, neutrons have a neutral charge, and electrons have a negative charge. Even though an atom is mostly empty space, protons and neutrons make up the main mass of an atom. Fun Facts
