Chemistry is a branch of science that focuses on the study of matter and interactions between substances.

There are five main branches of chemistry which involve, organic chemistry, biochemistry, inorganic chemistry, analytical chemistry, and physical chemistry, all of which look at the interactions between different substances. It typically involves numerous calculations and formulas but at it’s most basic level, it is structured around matter. The smallest of which are atoms. Atoms make up elements and these elements are sorted and categorized with the periodic table of elements.
