Atoms

Atoms are the smallest unit of matter that have properties of an individual element. Every atom has a nucleus, containing protons and neturons, and a electrons. Even though an atom is mostly empty space, protons and neturons make up the main mass of an atom. Fun Facts

Elements

Elements are pure subastances that have specific properties at specific temperatures. They consist of atoms that all have the same number of protons. The number of protons in an atom defines the element.

Periodic Table

The periodic table is how chemical elements are presented, ordered by their given atomic number, their number of protons, and grouped by their reactivity, etc.
