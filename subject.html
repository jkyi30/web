Atoms

Atoms are the smallest unit of matter that have properties of an individual element. Every atom has a nucleus, containing protons and neutrons, and a electrons. Even though an atom is mostly empty space, protons and neutrons make up the main mass of an atom. Fun Facts

Elements

Elements are pure subastances that have specific properties at specific temperatures. They consist of atoms that all have the same number of protons. The number of protons in an atom defines the element. The number of protons also defines the elements atomic number, which is useful when looking at a periodic table.

Periodic Table

The periodic table is how chemical elements are presented, ordered by their given atomic number and grouped by their reactivity, etc.
