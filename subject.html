Atoms

Atoms are the smallest unit of matter that have properties of an individual element. They consist of protons, neutrons, and electrons.

Elements

Elements are pure subastances that have specific properties at specific temperatures. They consist of atoms that all have the same number of protons.

Periodic Table

The periodic table is how chemical elements are presented, ordered by their given atomic number and their reactivity, etc.
